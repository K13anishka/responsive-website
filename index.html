India

Capital: New Delhi
Official languages: Hindi, English
Population: Over 1.4 billion
Area: 3,287,263 km²
Currency: Indian Rupee (INR)
Independence day: August 15, 1947
Brief info:

India, located in South Asia, is the world’s largest democracy and the seventh-largest country by area. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse landscapes, and vibrant traditions. India is famous for the Taj Mahal, its colorful festivals, varied cuisine, and as the birthplace of major world religions such as Hinduism, Buddhism, Jainism, and Sikhism.
